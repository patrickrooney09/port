Patrick Rooney

Software Engineer

    Patrick is a software engineer and award-nominated actor based in Brooklyn, New York. He recently completed Fullstack Academy's highly selective Future Code Web Development Fellowship program on a full scholarship, where he gained proficiency the PERN stack, among other technologies.

    Prior to his foray into tech, Patrick spent a decade honing his craft as an actor, performing in various plays and musicals, including the Les Miserables National Tour and leading regional productions across the country. When he's not coding, Patrick can be found playing chess, running, baking bread, watching his beloved Chicago sports teams, or enjoying an IPA.
